Why Visit Tadoba?

🐅 Tiger Capital

Home to over 115 tigers, Tadoba offers one of the highest chances of tiger sightings in India. The park's dense forest and diverse terrain provide perfect habitat for these magnificent predators.

🌿 Rich Biodiversity

Beyond tigers, discover 195 bird species, leopards, sloth bears, wild dogs, and countless other wildlife species in their natural habitat across 1,727 square kilometers.

🏞️ Scenic Beauty

Experience pristine wilderness with bamboo forests, grasslands, and the serene Tadoba Lake. The park's landscape changes dramatically with seasons, offering unique experiences year-round.

📸 Photography Paradise

Capture stunning wildlife moments and breathtaking landscapes. The park's diverse ecosystems provide countless opportunities for nature photography enthusiasts.

🚙 Safari Adventures

Choose from multiple safari zones including Moharli, Kolsa, Pangdi, and Agarzari. Each zone offers unique wildlife experiences and different terrains to explore.

🏨 Comfortable Stay

Stay in eco-friendly resorts and forest lodges that blend comfort with nature. Experience authentic jungle hospitality while supporting conservation efforts.

Location & Getting Here
